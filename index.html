Injection Moulding Machine Motherboard

Welcome to my portfolio! Here, I showcase some of my favourite projects and provide some insight into my decision making process for designing circuit boards.

Microcontroller: ATmega2560

Communication Protocols: USB, UART, I2C, SPI, Analog Signal

Number of Copper Layers: 4

Purpose: Control of Injection Moulding Machine Functionality; Inteface with Peripheral Daughter Board

This circuit board was part of the overall embedded system for Structur3D Printing's newest product, the Inj3ctor. The goal of the product is to provide a desktop solution to small-scale or prototype injection moulding needs. Thus, the system required the following functionality:

Stepper Motor Control, Extrusion Pressure Measuring, Display Capabilities, User Control, Data Storage & USB Interface
